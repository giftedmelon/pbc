PALLAS'S CAT

NAME

Pallas's cat (manul)

HABITAT

Deserts and rocky, mountainous regions from Tibet to Siberia

CLASSIFICATION SPECIES

SIZE

45 to 60 cm (from head to bottom) with 30 cm tale, 3kg

ECOLOGY

Usually, they have from 2 to 10 kittens, they are born blind and completely helpless. Only female manuls educate the kittens. Mainly feed on rodents and pikas. They are nocturnal. Their fur is the thickest out of all wild cats adapting its habitat whose temperature drops as low as -50 degree.

CULTURAL REPRESENTATION

Popular as an internet meme among Russians since 2012 as an image of unfriendly looking with the caption “Pet me”
